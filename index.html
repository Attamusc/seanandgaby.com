We're getting married in days!

Circa 2004. high school sweethearts, kindred spirits, & best friends. fell in love at the 9th grade home coming dance, even though i had a crush on her since 6th. too nervous to dance together, instead instant messaged each other for hours after the dance talking about how he like my hair because it is “curlyish.” every memory is together. proms, graduations, birthdays, & the first day of our big kid jobs. our love is simple & pure. we love silly movies, picnics, & take out. we enjoy being with our families & vacationing in disney world. looking forward to growing old together & living to be 100. we cherish each other, & realize how blessed we are. best advice was nanny’s: “you only have so many jackpots in life.” she was so right!

Everything is clearer when you're in love.

John Lennon
